About This Site

This site lists papers related to the topic of secure computation posted on IACR's eprint archive since September 2016.

The term secure computation is here rather loosely defined as whatever I find relevant. This includes a number of topics in cryptography such as Multi-Party Computation, Fully Homomorphic Encryption, Oblivious RAM and so on.

I keep the list as a way for me to stay somewhat up to date with new developments in this area, and to have an easy way find papers. The list is generated manually by going over the eprint RSS feed a few times a week. If you feel like I missed a paper let me know, and I will consider adding it to the list.
